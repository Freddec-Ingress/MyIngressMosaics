We need you!

Become a mosaic hunter

MIM site is a collaborative site, so we need mosaic hunters in every country. You love missions, you love mosaics, please join us! No need to be level 16 to be a good mosaic hunter ;)
How to become a mosaic hunter?
Sign in/Sign up to MIM site ; you could sign in with Facebook
How to register missions to MIM site?
Install our Chrome extension MIM Hunting Tool.
Once installed you could register missions in 1 click! A contextual help is embedded.
How to create mosaics to MIM site?
Go to Registration page ; your registered missions will be displayed.
Select some missions, verify data and mosaic image preview, then click on "Create" button.
That's it! You have just created your first mosaic to MIM site.
